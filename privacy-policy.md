# Privacy Policy for Spoken Subtitles

**Last Updated:** January 30, 2026

## Overview
Spoken Subtitles ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension processes subtitle data locally in your browser and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

## Data Collection
This extension does **NOT** collect, store, or transmit:
- Personal information
- Browsing history
- Subtitle content
- User data of any kind

## How It Works
- All subtitle processing happens locally on your device
- Text-to-speech conversion uses your browser's built-in speech synthesis
- OCR functionality (when enabled) processes images locally
- No internet connection required for core functionality

## Permissions Used
- **storage**: Saves your voice and rate preferences locally
- **activeTab**: Allows the extension to work on the current tab (required for content scripts)

## Third-Party Services
- Uses Tesseract.js library for OCR (open source, processes locally)
- No external APIs or cloud services

## Contact
For privacy concerns, please contact us through our GitHub repository: https://github.com/noly24/spoken-subtitles

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy occasionally. Check our GitHub repository for the latest version.
